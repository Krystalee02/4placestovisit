1. Japan

Japan is a country like no other, if you had to make one last trip it would definitely be Japan. Tokyo being its capital It is home to incredible fauna and has one of the best transportation in the world (bullet train), as well as being loved by anime lovers and their culture. It has a super incredible gastronomy, its main ingredient is fish, since Japan is a peninsula, it is surrounded by water, so its primary diet is based on seafood. it is also popular because of its beautiful streets filled with colors as well as their shrines, that holds years of history. traveling to Japan

Shrines and Temples

one of the many activities you should defenitely do, is visit the temples and shrines. Having the opportunity to visit a temple is an unique experience.

City Life

If wanting to experience something more fun and upbeat, then the city is a great option, you have a lot to choose from, themed-cafes and themed hotels, you have a great variety of food, not to forget you also have the otaku corner.

Coastal Cities

Coastal cities are a great option if you want to experience something calm and spend time on the beach or maybe the peaceful live of residents. some coastal cities are Osaka, Nagoya, Okinawa.
