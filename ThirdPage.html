2. Korea

South Korea, a country in East Asia. It occupies the southern portion of the Korean peninsula. Being well known for its harmonic culture, and its music also known as K-pop. if you are planning to travel to korea you must visit these places.

Seoul

Seoul is the main attraction due to the fact that a lot of companies either for beuty or technology are established there, furthermore kpop idols also are a big part of why seoul is popular, you may found one at the nearest coffe shop near you!

Busan

Busan is also popular because is a coastal city also because of hot springs, nature reserves, and events such as the city's renowned international film festival held each fall, something you should not miss for sure!

Gwanghwamun Square

Gwanghwamun Square is one of the coolest, most historic, artistic, and beautiful parts of Seoul. It’s where you’ll find the epic statues of King Sejong and General Yi Sun-sin, as well as the Sejong Center and the grand Gyeongbokgung Palace. you will also have the chance to rent and wear their traditional clothes and take some pictures.
